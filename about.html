What is the Bhagavad Gita?

The Bhagavad Gita is a 700-verse Hindu scripture that is part of the epic Mahabharata. It presents a conversation between Prince Arjuna and Lord Krishna, offering profound insights on duty, righteousness, and the path to spiritual liberation.

🧘‍♀️

Mental Wellness Through Gita

The Gita addresses universal human concerns: anxiety, purpose, duty, and inner peace. Its teachings provide practical wisdom for managing stress, finding clarity, and cultivating mental resilience in daily life.
