Guided Breathing Exercise

Follow the circle to practice mindful breathing

This simple yet powerful breathing technique helps calm your mind, reduce stress, and bring you into the present moment. The 4-4-4-4 pattern (breathe in for 4 seconds, hold for 4 seconds, breathe out for 4 seconds, hold for 4 seconds) is known as "box breathing" and is used by athletes, military personnel, and meditation practitioners worldwide.
